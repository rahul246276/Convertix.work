FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before reaching out to us.

Is there any fee to join Convertix?

Absolutely not. Joining Convertix.work is 100% free. There are no hidden charges, registration fees, or course fees for job applicants.

Do I need prior sales experience?

No experience is required. Our 30-day training program is designed for complete beginners and will make you job-ready from scratch.

How soon will I be contacted after applying?

Our team typically reaches out within 24 hours of receiving your application. Make sure your phone number and email are correct.

Can students and housewives apply?

Yes! Our platform is specifically designed for students, housewives, and unemployed individuals looking for flexible income opportunities.

What is the difference between Normal and Premium work?

Normal work is the standard track — great for beginners. Premium work gives you access to our high-performance team, advanced support, and higher commission tiers.

When do I start earning?

You can start earning from day one of going live. Most members make their first commission within the first two weeks after training completion.
